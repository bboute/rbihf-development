# Style Guide for Defensie Website

This document outlines the key styling elements observed in the provided Defensie website design, including colors, typography, spacing, and component-specific styles.

## Color Palette

- **Primary Dark Blue/Green:** `#00576B` (Used for main buttons, active states, and certain background sections)
- **Accent Red/Salmon:** `#E65C6B` (Used for secondary buttons, highlights, and interactive elements)
- **Light Beige/Off-White:** `#F5F2EB` (Dominant background color for content sections)
- **Dark Text/Background:** `#1A1A1A` (Used for main headings, body text, and hero section background)
- **Light Gray/Text:** `#A0A0A0` (Used for subtle text, borders, and inactive states)
- **Dark Green/Teal:** `#007B8F` (Used for specific interactive elements and text)

## Typography

- **Font Family:** Appears to be a sans-serif font, possibly a system font or a custom one similar to Open Sans or Lato.
- **Headings**
  - Large, bold, dark text (`#1A1A1A`).
  - Font size varies, with the hero section heading being the largest.
- **Body Text:**
  - Generally dark gray (`#1A1A1A`) or a slightly lighter shade for less prominent text.
  - Readable font size, likely around 16-18px.
- **Navigation/Button Text:**
  - White or light text on dark backgrounds, dark text on light backgrounds.
  - Often uppercase or title case, with medium to bold weight.
- **Links:**
  - Often styled with the accent red/salmon color (`#E65C6B`) or the primary dark blue/green (`#00576B`) when active or hovered.

## Spacing and Layout

- **Generous White Space:** Ample padding and margins are used to create a clean, uncluttered look.
- **Consistent Vertical Rhythm:** Vertical spacing between sections and elements appears consistent.
- **Grid-based Layout:** Content is often organized in columns (e.g., 2-column for text and image, 3-column for components/cards).
- **Full-width Sections:** Many sections span the full width of the viewport, with content constrained to a maximum width in the center.

## Component-Specific Styles

### Buttons
- **Primary Button**
  - Background: Primary Dark Blue/Green (`#00576B`)
  - Text: White
  - Border-radius: Slightly rounded corners (e.g., 4-8px)
  - Padding: Generous horizontal and vertical padding.
- **Secondary Button**
  - Background: Accent Red/Salmon (`#E65C6B`)
  - Text: White
  - Border-radius: Slightly rounded corners (e.g., 4-8px)
  - Padding: Generous horizontal and vertical padding.
- **Outline Buttons**
  - Background: Transparent
  - Border: 1-2px solid white or primary dark blue/green.
  - Text: White or primary dark blue/green.
  - Border-radius: Slightly rounded corners.

### Navigation Bar
- **Background:** Dark (`#1A1A1A`)
- **Text:** White or light gray.
- **Hover/Active States:** Subtle underlines or background changes.
- **Search/Language/Menu Icons:** Often in a light beige/off-white background with rounded corners.

### Cards/Sections with Images
- **Image Overlays:** Images often have a colored overlay (e.g., primary dark blue/green, accent red/salmon) with varying opacity, sometimes with a subtle pattern.
- **Text on Images:** White, bold text for titles, positioned at the bottom left.
- **Rounded Corners:** Some cards or image containers have slightly rounded corners.

### Input Fields/Forms
- **Background:** Light beige/off-white or white.
- **Border:** Subtle gray border.
- **Border-radius:** Slightly rounded corners.
- **Placeholder Text:** Light gray.

### Icons
- **Arrow Icons:** Often circular with a light background and dark arrow, or dark background with a light arrow.
- **Checkmark/Info Icons:** Used with the primary dark blue/green or accent red/salmon colors.

## Overall Aesthetic

The website conveys a professional, modern, and trustworthy image through its use of a limited, strong color palette, clear typography, and ample white space. The design is clean and functional, with a focus on readability and clear calls to action. The use of subtle patterns and image overlays adds visual interest without distracting from the content.
